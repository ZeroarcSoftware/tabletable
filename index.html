tabletable

tabletable is a simple table component written purely in React. It is the result of our frustrations with other React tables that are overly prescriptive in how they must be used and styled. tabletable intentionally leaves styling and functionality up to the integrator to make it easy to customize and integrate into complex applications.

tabletable takes any sort of data structure and allows you to define your columns' markup and data projections as you like. It is also server-side rendering friendly.

For more information on use and contribution, check out the Readme.
